Get to know Leo

Where it all started

In 2015, I decided to get a certificate in IT administration to better support my family. I worked a full time retail job while going to training in the evenings. Midway through training I got an IT helpdesk job. From there I was able to finish my training and continue expanding my career in IT.

It's ironic because growing up I always dreamed of going to college and getting a engineering degree and working where my dad worked at for 20+ years. Although it didn't work out the way I dreamed and I ended up going down a different path I somehow still ended up at the place where my dad worked and currently hold a position with a engineering title except it's different kind of engineer.

My Why

My passion is to serve and help those in need and lead by example. I want every encounter with everyone to be a crazy amazing experience and empower them by sharing knowledge.

Where I'm at

As I have continued my career in IT in the last 10 years I have learned so much. I've learned that in IT there will always be constant change and although at times I can be reluctant to change I have challenged myself to adapt and grow. I've made customer service and the customer experience the focal point in my career because I enjoy helping and serving.

My other passions and more about me
